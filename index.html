Cebu Panoramic Views Tour to Temple of Leah
Insert Image

Description

See the best panoramic views from top Cebu tourist spots with this day tour. This trip is perfect for photography enthusiasts as you will be visiting view decks that provide unobstructed views of Cebu. The tour also includes land transfers, free hotel pick-up and drop-off within select areas, entrance fees to each attraction, complimentary bottled water, and the service of a driver-guide. If you're staying in a hotel within Mandaue, Lapu-Lapu, or Cebu City, your guide will fetch you from your accommodation. Otherwise, you will have to meet up with your guide at an agreed-upon point. After meeting your guide, you will travel to your first stop of the day: the Taoist Temple. The temple sits at an elevation of 110 feet above sea level, giving you a scenic view of downtown Cebu. From there, you will go to the Temple of Leah. Often hailed as one of the most picturesque sites in Cebu, this temple features Greek-like architecture that resembles temples of ancient Greece. Following your visit here, you will be given time to eat lunch at a restaurant at your own expense. A recommended restaurant to try out is Terrazas de Flores. When you've finished your lunch, you will continue onto Little Kyoto. Here, you will find yourself transported to Japan as the property features Torii gates and other fixtures inspired by Japanese culture. On top of this, Little Kyoto has a viewing deck that gives you a beautiful view of the surrounding mountains. Afterward, you will go to Buwakan ni Alejandra. Prepare your smartphones or cameras as this is a vast flower garden that boasts colorful rows of fresh blooms. Finally, you will end your tour at the West 35 Eco Mountain Resort. The resort's main draw is a 360-degree view of the Balamban Mountains and the seaside of Tsuneishi.
